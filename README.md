# HR-Analytics-Dashboard-project-
This project was developed using Microsoft Excel for data preparation and Power BI for data modeling, visualization, and reporting.The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution designed to help HR professionals analyze workforce data, monitor employee trends, and make data-driven decisions
## Project Objective
Analyze employee demographics and workforce distribution.
Monitor employee attrition and retention rates.
Track department-wise employee performance.
Identify trends in hiring, promotions, and employee turnover.
Support strategic HR decision-making through data visualization.

## Process
1. Data Collection
Gathered HR employee data from Excel files.
Included information such as employee demographics, department, salary, performance, and attrition status.
2. Data Cleaning and Preparation (Excel)
Removed duplicate records.
Handled missing and inconsistent values.
Standardized data formats.
Created additional calculated fields where required.
3. Data Import into Power BI
Imported the cleaned Excel dataset into Power BI.
Verified data types and relationships.
Structured the data model for analysis.
4. Data Modeling
Created relationships between tables (if applicable).
Developed calculated columns and measures using DAX.
Built KPIs for workforce analysis.
5. Dashboard Development
Designed an interactive dashboard layout.
Added KPI cards for key HR metrics.
Created charts and visualizations for employee analysis.
Implemented slicers and filters for dynamic reporting.
6. Data Analysis
Analyzed employee demographics.
Evaluated attrition and retention trends.
Examined department-wise workforce distribution.
Assessed employee performance and satisfaction metrics.
7. Insight Generation
Identified high attrition departments.
Highlighted workforce diversity trends.
Evaluated employee engagement indicators.
Generated actionable HR insights for decision-making.
8. Dashboard Testing and Optimization
Validated calculations and visualizations.
Optimized report performance.
Improved user experience with interactive features.
9. Final Reporting
Published the completed dashboard.
Documented findings and business recommendations.
Shared insights through visual reports and dashboards.

## Project Insights
The organization has a diverse workforce across multiple departments, with some departments having significantly higher employee counts than others.
Employee attrition is concentrated in specific departments and job roles, indicating areas that may require improved retention strategies.
Employees with lower job satisfaction scores show a higher likelihood of leaving the organization.
Work-life balance and employee engagement appear to have a direct impact on retention rates.
Certain age groups and experience levels contribute more significantly to overall attrition.
Salary and compensation trends reveal opportunities to improve employee satisfaction and reduce turnover.
Performance analysis helps identify high-performing employees and potential candidates for promotion and leadership roles.
Gender distribution and workforce demographics provide valuable insights into diversity and inclusion initiatives.

## Final Conclusion

The HR Analytics Dashboard successfully transforms raw employee data into meaningful and actionable insights. By leveraging Excel for data preparation and Power BI for visualization, the dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of workforce performance, employee demographics, attrition trends, and organizational health.

The analysis highlights key factors influencing employee turnover, including job satisfaction, work-life balance, and departmental challenges. These insights enable HR teams and management to make informed decisions regarding employee retention, talent development, workforce planning, and organizational growth.

Overall, this project demonstrates the power of data analytics in Human Resource Management by helping organizations improve employee engagement, optimize HR processes, and support strategic business objectives through data-driven decision-making.
